In most emerging countries, SMEs represent the bulk of economic activity. They are unfortunately still not turned towards digital.
Yet their digital transformation is known to increase their economic efficiency.

The obstacles to digital adoption

Numerous obstacles to the adoption of digital still exist throughout the African continent.
Many studies of consulting firms highlight certain factors as being at the origin of this situation. These include:

⟶ Budgetary restrictions: effectively SMEs in Africa are often limited by their investment capacity;

⟶ Failure to understand the real contribution of digital to their activities;

⟶ Lack of ICT skills within these structures.
