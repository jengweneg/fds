From idea to proof of concept

If the idea is the foundation of any entrepreneurial project, it is still necessary to ensure the viability of such a project, that is to say, the ability to build a solid economic model based on the idea. Fiatope accompanies you to identify the right digital tool according to the maturation stage of your idea.
Before throwing oneself into the water, and spending a fortune to develop and launch ones idea or project, it is crucial to make sure beforehand that the underlying concept will meet the customers interest. For that, nothing better than to test the concept with a small panel of potential customers. This is called a "proof of concept". Will future customers understand the service? Is it easy and convenient to use? These are some of the questions that the entrepreneur must ask himself
It goes without saying that not all ideas can be tested on a small scale. However, whenever possible, it is essential to start with a proof of concept.
Many people who wish to start a business unfortunately stop at the idea stage, simply due to the lack of opportunity to put up a real proof of their idea, or lack of knowledge that this is possible with little financial means.
Yet today there are many ways, exploiting open source technologies in particular, to achieve a proof of concept or develop its idea first on a small scale.
Fiatope analyzes with you the best approach to test your concept and validate its relevance thanks to digital technologies.
Finally this stage is the best time to think about your economic model and validate it.

Development

Your concept has been tested on a very small scale (some people have tried it) and it is convincing with users and possibly investors. Moreover, you have validated at least your economic model. You must now move from concept to commercial product.
The development phase comes next. This is a crucial phase in the life of your project. Indeed, the product design choices you made with your team will be decisive for the future. The technical skills that you are going to surround are going to have to be selected with great care and the technologies that will constitute the framework of your system will have to be chosen with maximal pertinence.
Fiatope lets you benefit from all the expertise of its consultants in the field and recommends you the right digital tools to design the best product possible.

Service Launch

Be careful, it's not because development is finished that you can launch. A surprising number of additional actions await you. They should be carried out with utmost care and, once again, using the appropriate digital tools. After having legally registered your activity, you will have to find a brand name (if this has not been done upstream), reserve a domain name for your presence on the internet and your email communication, protect your concept if necessary, prepare yourself to invest social networks, find a hosting environment for your services to the extent that these are carried out online ...
With Fiatope, you can ensure that you are able to achieve these milestones thanks to the practice we have developed and the launch of our own projects.
