Fundraising

Kwendoo

Kwendoo is a web application intended to bring a community of friends, family members, colleagues, fans, followers....to easily contribute any given amount of money to fund an event, a cause, a ceremony, a project, a trip, funerals... Kwendoo is launched in Rwanda (www.kwendoo.rw) but also France (www.kwendoo.com) and has +5000 customers. Next country of launch is Cameroon

Fiatope

www.fiatope.com is a crowdfunding platform dedicated at helping young african entrepreneurs to fund their projects with the collective support of the diasporas from their countries in Europe. Fiatope targets small projects with an average seed capital need of 10000 USD. Fiatope Fund has been operating for one year and has so far funded 5 projects in Cameroun, Burkina Faso and Rwanda.

Ticketing

Tickects Kwendoo

Kwendoo Tickets is a web application that removes the burden of having to go to a selling point to buy or collect event tickets. With our service, the customer buys their ticket online and receives it on their phone. Event organizers get a simple and secure way of collecting revenues from tickets sales. Rwanda is our first launch http://tickets.kwendoo.rw Next country is Ivory Coast

Business Kwendoo

Kwendoo Business is our product to support the development of e-commerce. We work with banks and telcos to bring solutions for you to be able to easily collect mobile money as well as credit/debit cards payments trough your virtual POS. https://business.kwendoo.rw The solution addresses the payment of bookings, services, fees, bills, shop items... We also offer an API for integration to your e-commerce plaform
