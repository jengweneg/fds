ABOUT US

Fiatope Digital Solutions is at the same time a digital start-up and a consulting consulting firm specializing in supportingprofesionals and companies in the IT field as well as companies in need of digital tools for their development.

The Start-Up business is at the heart of our identity and we are first of all a company that aims to bring to to the market products and services aimed at making life easy for african citizens but also for african companies.

For this we build services in fields as diverse as digital finance, online payment, digital identity, wireless connectivity and the digital transformation of African SMEs.

The consulting activity was born out of the founder's desire to share his experience and his passion for the IT industry, the management of men and women for leading large projects in professional and personal development, as well for university education. Other high-level professionals have since joined the adventure to form a shock team of specialized consultants.

Unlike the traditional approach of consulting firms, Fiatope Consulting always goes for a deep knowledge of the company and its people. We focus on what happens on its field of play and we reflect on really lived situations.

We bring you real added value, based on our know-how, the expertise of our consultants and their recognized performance in France, Belgium and Europe in their field of activity
