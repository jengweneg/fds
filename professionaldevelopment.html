Professional Development

We have an always evolving training offer. It is organized around 2 modules :

Team management and personal development at preparation for recruitment.

These modules are not intended to be merely taught. Our intervenants/speakers generally comment real situation and bring participants to ask theirselves the right questions in the face of similar situations. Thus, the latter are more aware of the problems than if they were to be dispensed with a simple recital of pre-made formulas that would not being appropriate to their individual situation.
